# Ravenwood narrative rules

- The current episode-one canon in `docs/narrative-canon.md` overrides legacy dialogue files and archived scenes.
- The group «Семеро» exists only for the opening conflict. After Derek deletes it, it must stay locked and receive no new messages.
- After the group is deleted, public character interaction belongs in RavenFeed comments. Optional private branches are limited to Olivia, Mia and Derek; Brooke's finale demand is public.
- Mason and Tyler are silent after the Ravenwood Truth publication.
- Episode one ends after Olivia and Mia react and Brooke publicly demands an explanation.
- A character may use information only if it was public, shown in their chat, or explicitly relayed to them.
- Choices and trust must change tone, access, remembered boundaries or later reactions. Low trust may reduce optional content but must not block the main route.
- Before editing a scene, verify its place in `docs/branching-map.md` and the participants' knowledge at that point.
