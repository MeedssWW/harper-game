# Episode 1 canon

## Fixed premise

The player lives in another city, has never visited Ravenwood and has never met Harper Vance or her friends. Derek contacts the player because a message from Harper's phone contained only the player's number. It is not known who sent that message.

## Canonical order

1. Derek contacts the player and gradually explains the number.
2. Derek creates «Семеро», adds the player and visibly shares the screenshot of the message.
3. Brooke and Mason oppose Derek's decision. Tyler confirms only that the number on the screenshot matches; he does not treat it as proof.
4. The conflict breaks the group apart. Derek deletes it and the chat is locked.
5. Olivia apologizes privately and sends a RavenFeed invitation.
6. The player first sees ordinary Ravenwood posts and profiles. Brooke's search post appears after this first look.
7. Public character interaction continues in RavenFeed comments. An optional everyday exchange with Olivia can be skipped.
8. Mia writes privately. Everyone gets one everyday conversation; a warmer Harper memory opens only with sufficient Mia trust.
9. Notes open after the Mia conversations and record facts separately from uncertainty.
10. Derek sends four ordinary photos of Harper. A short, awkward everyday exchange about Ravenwood or football can follow; the player may decline it.
11. A conversation about Mia's jacket naturally prompts Mia to search old photos. She finds the folder copied from her old phone and remembers that Harper briefly used the device.
12. Mia reports the phone to the investigator and offers restricted access to the existing backup. The player's privacy choices are recorded.
13. The backup contains a deleted chat and a link to `VID_1842.mp4`. The contact and message text are damaged; Mia's testimony is not presented as technical proof.
14. Opening the external attachment compromises the player's device. The visible effects are chat/note access, camera access and loss of control.
15. Unknown appears only after the connection is interrupted. Their claims remain claims unless supported by the visible connection log.
16. Ravenwood Truth publishes the same camera image captured during the compromise and frames public facts without proving guilt.
17. Olivia and Mia react privately according to trust. Brooke demands an explanation publicly in RavenFeed. Mason and Tyler remain silent.
18. Episode one ends immediately after that demand.

## Excluded legacy events

Episode one does not include the Larks window clue, a dark-green sedan, the old bridge as a surveillance route, North Yard, the old station investigation, restored sticker analysis, Derek following Harper, a body, a death confirmation or the legacy finale.
